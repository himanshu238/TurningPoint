Our Background

We take an opportunity in introducing Turning Point Studies Consultants (TPSC) providing personalized consultancy to students as well as parents, at all stages of education, from school to University level, and have guided more than thousands of students for admission into the academic institutions of different countries of the world. Since 1993 TPSC has been aspiring Indian Students from different locations of India through its offices at New Delhi and Haryana and successfully turning the dreams into a reality of the aspiring Indian students having the desire to study overseas.
TPSC is officially associated with over 400 universities worldwide including American, British, Australian, Canadian, and other English speaking countries accredited by the western association of schools and colleges affiliated with the universities known for Academic Excellence.
TPSC is one centre where we guide students for Foreign Education by offering Globally Accepted Courses in accordance with their present qualification standard, both Academic and Professional, at the time of admission who eventually becomes skilled enough and are absorbed by the World Class Corporate Business Houses.
TPSC welcomes applications from different social, educational, and cultural backgrounds regardless of race, colour, and religion. Our core business is to guide students for admission and study permit applications.

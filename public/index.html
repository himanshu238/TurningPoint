Why Choose Us?

Turning Point Studies Consultants (TPSC), founded in 1993 and is the part of today, stand on the strong credence of being an expert consulting group, headquartered in New Delhi and registered office in Karnal, Haryana. It was incorporated in 2001 by the Registrar of Companies Delhi and Haryana, Ministry of Corporate Affairs, with an aim to assist students from India for higher education abroad. TPSC is a leader in international student placement services. For more than 27 years, TPSC has played a significant role in the education industry. During this time, Turning Point has counseled thousands of students for placement in quality institutions in Australia, the United Kingdom, the United States of America,Canada, New Zealand, Singapore, and European countries.

F A Q

Is there a fee to see an advisor at Turning Point?

TPSC services are free for students. There is no cost to students to book an appointment with TPSC. Students do not pay an agent fee to TPSC to study overseas. There may be some small fees such as courier fees or document verification fees. For more information, phone or speak to your nearest TPSC counsellor or complete our enquiry form.
